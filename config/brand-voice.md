# Brand Voice — Petacular

## Business
**Name:** Petacular
**Industry:** pet products
**Services:** premium pet food, toys, accessories, bedding, grooming products, health supplements for dogs and cats

## Tone of Voice
- Professional and trustworthy
- Friendly and approachable
- Clear and jargon-free (unless writing for industry peers)
- Confident — we know our craft

## Writing Style
- Short sentences preferred
- Use active voice
- Lead with customer benefit, not features
- Include local references where relevant
